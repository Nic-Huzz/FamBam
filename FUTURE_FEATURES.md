# FamBam Future Features

A roadmap of potential features to enhance family connection and engagement.

---

## High Impact / Quick Wins

### Call Button in Connections
- [ ] Add phone number field to user profiles
- [ ] Display call button next to each family member in Connections tab
- [ ] Tap to initiate call via `tel:` link
- [ ] Track calls made through the app

### Weekly Family Digest
- [ ] Email summary of family activity (posts, challenges, points)
- [ ] Configurable delivery day (e.g., Sunday morning)
- [ ] Highlights: top posts, who earned badges, connection stats
- [ ] Opt-in/out in Profile settings

### Birthday/Anniversary Reminders
- [ ] Add birthday and anniversary fields to profiles
- [ ] Push notification on the day
- [ ] "Upcoming" section in app (next 30 days)
- [ ] Auto-suggest "Celebrate a win" challenge for birthdays

### Quick Reactions on Feed
- [ ] Double-tap to react (default emoji: 仇벒잺)
- [ ] Haptic feedback on reaction
- [ ] Heart animation overlay
- [ ] Reduce friction for engagement

---

## Engagement Boosters

### Family Milestones
- [ ] Track collective family achievements
- [ ] Milestones: 100/500/1000 total family points
- [ ] Milestones: 50/100/500 total posts
- [ ] Celebratory banner in feed when achieved
- [ ] Family badge displayed on all members' profiles

### Challenge Suggestions
- [ ] AI-powered personalized prompts
- [ ] "You haven't shared good news in 3 weeks"
- [ ] "Call Mom? It's been 12 days"
- [ ] Display in Challenges page or as push notifications
- [ ] Based on user's challenge completion history

### Throwback Posts
- [ ] "On this day" memories from 1 year ago
- [ ] Display in feed or dedicated section
- [ ] Option to reshare with new comment
- [ ] Push notification: "See what you shared 1 year ago"

### Family Polls
- [ ] Create quick polls for group decisions
- [ ] "Where should we go for Christmas?"
- [ ] "What day works for family call?"
- [ ] Results visible to all family members
- [ ] Points for participating in polls

---

## Connection Deepeners

### Video Messages
- [ ] Record short video updates (15-60 seconds)
- [ ] In-app recording with front/back camera
- [ ] Video posts in feed with playback
- [ ] More personal than text-only updates
- [ ] Compress videos for fast upload

### Shared Family Calendar
- [ ] Family-wide calendar view
- [ ] Add events: visits, calls, gatherings
- [ ] See who's free when
- [ ] Sync with device calendar (optional)
- [ ] RSVP functionality

### Family Tree View
- [ ] Visual map of family relationships
- [ ] Define relationships (parent, sibling, cousin, etc.)
- [ ] Connection stats overlaid on tree
- [ ] Tap member to see their profile
- [ ] Interactive zoom/pan

### Group Challenges
- [ ] Challenges requiring multiple family members
- [ ] "The whole family visits Grandma this month"
- [ ] "Everyone posts a photo this week"
- [ ] Progress bar showing family completion
- [ ] Bonus points for completing as a group

---

## Gamification Extensions

### Seasonal Events
- [ ] Special badges for holidays (Christmas, Easter, Thanksgiving)
- [ ] Limited-time challenges with bonus points
- [ ] Themed UI elements during events
- [ ] "Holiday Hero" badge for most festive family member

### Family Leagues
- [ ] Opt-in competition against other families
- [ ] Weekly/monthly leaderboard
- [ ] Anonymous family names for privacy
- [ ] Rewards for top-performing families
- [ ] Categories: most connected, most active, etc.

### Achievement Unlock Notifications
- [ ] Push notification when badge earned
- [ ] Celebratory animation in-app
- [ ] Share badge to feed automatically (optional)
- [ ] Sound effect for unlocks

### Points Decay
- [ ] Gentle pressure to stay engaged
- [ ] Streak breaks after 2 weeks of inactivity
- [ ] Warning notification before streak breaks
- [ ] "Comeback Kid" badge for returning users
- [ ] Configurable in settings (on/off)
